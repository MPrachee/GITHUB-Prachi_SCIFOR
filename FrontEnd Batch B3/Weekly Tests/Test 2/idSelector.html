Hanging Plants That Can Improve Air Quality

Boston Fern: It is known for its ability to filter polluntants like formaldehyde, xylene, & toluene from the air. Its lush feathery fronds make it a classic choice for hanging baskets indoors on patios.

Peace Lily: The peace Lily is famous for removing a variety of toxins, including ammonia, benzene, formaldehyde, & trichloroethylene. Though not a traditional hanging plant, it can be grown in hanging planters for its elegant arching leaves & white blooms.
